Description

Many young women and teenage girls struggle with self-hatred, comparison, lust, loneliness, depression, anxiety, and trauma. Unfortunately, these issues are progressively becoming worse, and society is not properly aware of them or adequately equipped to deal with them.

To combat this, my friend Lyissa and I started an organization called My Sister's Revolution. Through conferences, we share our testimonies and experiences of the redemptive power of Jesus Christ and our identities and potential as beloved daughters of Heavenly Parents.

We have hosted three conferences, all amassing over 40-120 attendees of girls and their guardians. We have been blessed to have wonderful speakers and musicians present, including:

  1. Sarah Sun (Miss Utah 2023)
    • Inspiring keynote speeches
    • Role model for young women
  2. Sara Arkell (the Piano Gal)
  3. Tamara McFadden (Young Women General Advisory Council Member)

Through these conferences, we have been able to create community and sisterhood, promote healing, speak openly about pornography, mental health issues, and other common struggles, and see the hand of God displayed.

Conference Planning

For each conference, we put together a team of 8-12 like-minded girls and young women, rent an appropriate venue, plan decorations, secure necessary technology, find willing and talented presenters, advertise, and manage the event.

I have also spoken at a Girl’s Camp and a fireside.
